About

Who are we?

"AzeContent" is a collection of necessary content in the Azerbaijani language in a single space for the development of youth and is an online platform created for foreigners to learn our language more easily and practically. Azerbaijani youth We are aware of the importance of adapting our language to the requirements of the day, not the century. That's why we are with you now!

Our Mission

Providing people, especially young people, with content in our language is a language that hinders their development to remove the obstacle, to make our language more popular among young people. Not just for local youth as well to make the Azerbaijani language accessible to foreigners. In short, to develop our own language and our own is to develop in our language.

Our Vision

- Crossing borders in our own language,
- Recognition among local youth,
- Implement unrealized projects,
- To become the leading platform,
- Finally, to build our country in our own language.
